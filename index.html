    I’m a multidisciplinary creative who has a keen eye for design. My curiosity has always led me to explore new interests, whether it’s learning how to sew or how to produce music, I'm constantly creating. To be a dynamic designer I believe understanding people is the first step to understanding a problem, that’s why my positivity and ability to empathize with others allows me to view situations from a new and unique perspective.
